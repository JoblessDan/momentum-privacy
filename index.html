MOMENTUM

Fitness tracking for athletes and coaches

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Momentum ("we", "our", or "us") is a fitness tracking application that helps athletes log workouts, track progress, and work with personal trainers. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using Momentum you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide the app's functionality:

Category Data Purpose
Account Email address, first name, last name, username Account creation, authentication, and profile display
Fitness data Workout sessions, exercise names, weights, reps, planned workouts Core app functionality — logging and tracking your training
Identifiers Internal user ID Linking your data across devices and sessions
Notifications Device push token (if you grant permission) Sending optional workout reminders

We do not collect location data, contacts, photos, browsing history, financial information, or any sensitive personal data.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and maintain your account
  • To store and display your workout history and progress statistics
  • To enable the trainer–client relationship feature (trainers can view their clients' workout data)
  • To send transactional emails such as email verification and password reset links
  • To send push notifications if you choose to enable them

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sell it to any third party.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Google Cloud SQL (europe-west1 region). The app backend runs on Google Cloud Run. All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS. Passwords are hashed using Argon2id before storage and are never stored in plain text. Access tokens have a short lifetime and refresh tokens are rotated on each use.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

  • Google Cloud Platform — infrastructure hosting (Cloud Run, Cloud SQL, Secret Manager). Google Cloud Privacy Notice →
  • Resend — transactional email delivery (verification and password reset emails only). Resend Privacy Policy →

No analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or other data-sharing third parties are used in this app.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data associated with your account — including your profile, workout history, and authentication tokens — is permanently deleted from our systems.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data via the Profile screen in the app
  • Delete your account and all associated data from the Profile screen
  • Export your data — contact us and we will provide a copy

8. Children's Privacy

Momentum is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
